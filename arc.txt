Based on your project guideline, your title is Medical Store Stock and Billing System. The subject specifically requires HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, PHP, MySQL, and Web Server.
1. Project Overview
Project Title
Medical Store Stock and Billing System
Aim
Develop a web-based system to manage medicine inventory, sales billing, suppliers, customers, and stock monitoring for a pharmacy/medical store.
Objectives
Manage medicine inventory efficiently.
Generate bills automatically.
Track medicine stock levels.
Monitor expired medicines.
Maintain supplier records.
Generate sales reports.
Reduce manual paperwork.
